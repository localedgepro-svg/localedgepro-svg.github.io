About

Rooted Home Journal is a small, independent publication about the unglamorous but expensive parts of owning a home — water heaters, roofs, foundations, basements, and everything that quietly fails when you're not looking.

What sets our writing apart is that we lean on public data. When we say water hardness shortens a heater's life or that a county floods often, we're pointing at the same records utilities, the EPA, FEMA, and the U.S. Census Bureau publish — not vibes. The goal is simple: help you make an informed decision instead of a rushed or fearful one.

We're not contractors and we don't sell services. When a job is genuinely worth a professional, we say so and point you toward finding a qualified local one. Everything here is editorial, and we're not affiliated with any government agency or brand.
